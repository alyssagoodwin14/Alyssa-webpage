My Trip to Punta Cana

For a week in July this summer, I visited Punta Cana, Dominican Republic. We stayed at a resort that had a pool, beach, water park, restaurants, and shops. My favorite part of the resort was the beach and pool. While we were there, we went snorkeling and tubing. I love snorkeling and seeing all of the fish was really cool. I really enjoyed spending time with my family and seeing a new place. I would definitely recommend a vacation to Punta Cana if you can't decide where to go. The beach and weather is amazing. You will love it!
